Gathering of Ancients


The Tree of Life slumbered

And the Druid's Rock was tranquil

Not a hero nor villain

Drew blades nor cast incantation

~~~

But the Valley remembers...

A prayer for good fortune

For heroes

And friends

For adventurers

Oh, so many adventurers

Creatures of the night...

And slayers of men!

The rangers of the wood

And merry company indeed

The seekers of lore

Treasure hunters

Healers

And brigands!

To each came glory

Or fame

Or renown

Or dread...

But surely each met battle

Or narrow escape.

That in the end, the best thing anyone could hope for was a good death!

Greetings ye olde adventurers! I am going to host a Games of Adventure Friends & Family event this summer, and you're cordially invited to join. It won't be a typical GOA game day, but will instead be a gathering in "town" at the main shelter, where we can enjoy each others' company, eat (potluck-style), drink (non alcoholic), be merry, regale one another with stories of the old days, and maybe... just maybe... have an open space for those who want to spar! Costuming is highly encouraged. I expect many at such a Gathering of Ancients may likely have a youngin' or two, so please feel free to bring your families along. I am hoping we can make it a fun day of revelries, to include pop-up vendors for anyone who has medieval-hi-fantasy gear for sale or trade, and entertainment if there are any performers who can join us! PLEASE share the message as far and wide as possible. Shake the old tree branches and let anyone who has played GOA in the past know about Sunday August 25th. Bring a friend! I greatly look forward to seeing YOU there!!

Sincerely, Alex George



DIRECTIONS

"Town" / Main Shelter, Ravenna Park, Seattle WA
