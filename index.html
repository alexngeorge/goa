Gathering of Ancients


The Tree of Life slumbered

And the Druid's Rock was tranquil

Not a hero nor villain

Drew blades nor cast incantation

~~~

But the Valley remembers...

A prayer for good fortune

Of heroes

And friends

Of adventurers

Oh, so many adventurers

Creatures of the night...

And slayers of men!

Of rangers of the wood

And merry company indeed

The seekers of lore

Treasure hunters

Healers

And brigands!

To each came glory

Or fame

Or renown

Or dread...

But surely each meet battle

Or narrow escape.

In the end, the best thing anyone could hope for was a good death!
